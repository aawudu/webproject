Background

Pinnacle Edge Construction (PEC) believes in providing our clients with smart and connected homes because they offer convenience, comfort, peace of mind, and comparatively cheaper maintenance over time. Our designs and engineering provide a sense of balance, improve quality of life, are economically rewarding, and stand the test of time. We go further to bring our designs to life by constructing our buildings with the best materials, tools, techniques, and craft.


Our Smart Homes are incorporated with control systems, which provide integrated, centralized control of two or more individual systems. These systems may include environmental control systems (liquid waste, air conditioning and ventilation), household appliances (clothes dryers, washing machines, microwaves, etc.), consumer electronics (TV, radio, audio-video equipment,) and Building components (blinds, curtains, windows, doors, etc.). Our upgraded smart home concept is accessible remotely via the internet, allowing for seamless interactivity with homeowners.


Our business philosophy involves harnessing available tried and tested technologies to improve the interaction between homes, inhabitants, and the environment. We work with clients to provide a comprehensive solution to meet their construction needs and budget. We offer quality building design and architectural services, construction and project management services, and heavy-duty earthmoving equipment hiring services.


Based on our extensive industry experience in the construction sector and appreciation that homeownership is a huge capital investment for our clients, our team of experts applies succinct knowledge, creative workmanship, and an array of tools to deliver excellent output all the time. Using a three-point based approach of a structured client budget, available technology, and environmental consciousness, we are able to design and construct our building with minimal cost overruns. This approach enables our business to access the best quality construction materials for our work. Over the years, PEC has procured a fleet of quality construction equipment, which are also available for use by other contractors on a hiring basis.


Key benefits of our business orientation toward smart and connected homes include managing all of your home devices from one place, thus keeping all of the technology in your home connected through one interface. This allows for flexibility as our smart home systems easily support new devices, appliances, and other technology. Additionally, Home security is enhanced when integrated with the surveillance system. The ability to remotely control the home helps in improving home management considerably. By employing technology in this manner, energy efficiency and appliance functionality are improved significantly, and the data gathered by the system helps in providing home management insights. Our building approach offers long-term satisfaction with little impact on the environment while saving you time and resources.


The PEC engineers will be glad to assist you in designing, engineering, constructing, or purchasing one of our already built-up smart or connected homes.



Strategic Positioning

The local housing construction sector is characterized by the increasing cost of building or acquiring a home, high energy consumption and home maintenance cost, and the growing availability of various sustainable and adaptive communication technology. Our management team is strategically positioning the business in the smart and connected home segment of the market. In line with this thought process, the company has placed the following vision, mission, and values as its guide.
