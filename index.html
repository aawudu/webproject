Services

Design and Architectural Services

The PEC architectural and engineering team has experience spanning both domestic, commercial, and industrial facilities. We work with our clients to provide diverse designs that are spatially balanced, functionally optimal, environmentally durable with a comparatively lower cost of maintenance. Our design and architectural services output include bills of quantities, structural engineering, detailed drawing, and certification of drawing.

Construction and Project Management Services

Our construction department has experienced professionals who will help bring to life our clients' dreams and desires. Our expertise span across :
1. Domestic building works;
2. Commercial and industrial facilities;
3. Warehousing;
4. Drainage works;
5. Pavement and landscaping;
6. Installation of solar panels, batteries, inverters, biogas plants with its accessories, Smart and Intel systems, security and alarms systems.

Heavy Duty Equipment Hiring

PEC has progressively acquired series of earthmoving equipment deployed during various project executions and is available for rent by other construction firms on a need basis. Some of the available earthmoving equipment include:
1. Tipper trucks;
2. Excavators;
3. Backhoes;
4. Concrete mixers.
